Who is Sam Riley?

About Me

My name is Sam Riley. I was born and raised in Indianapolis, IN. After graduating from Lawrence North HS in 2009, I attended Indiana University until 2013, receiving a BA in History. Additionally I hold an Indiana Real Estate Broker's License, and am 3A certified pesticide applicator through the Office of the Indiana State Chemist. I am currently a student at The Iron Yard.

Interest

My favorite activities include coding, Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, reading, and playing video games.

Coding

I began teaching myself HTML, CSS, and Python in 2016 before discovering The Iron Yard. I began the Front End Engineering cohort in September, with a focus on JavaScript.

Brazilian Jiu Jitsu

I began training Brazilian Jiu Jitsu in 2014 at Fishers BJJ & Boxing. I am currently a white belt under professor Jason Kesler, and professor James Clingerman. Strong points include half guard, side control, and of course escapes. Favorite submissions include collar chokes, and kimura/Americana.
